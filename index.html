About us

MG, as the name suggests ''Mother's Love" is committed to delivering love and happiness to helpless humans of the society & environment. MG Foundation is formed by young team who wants to encourage people to come forward and join with us for this great initiative. Everything we are doing to provide good quality food,clothes,women empowerment and better society. MG foundation is a growing foundation which design to work in everywhere, where no help can reach, MG Foundation will reach. The overwhelming response and unconditional support of the volunteers have always been our inspiration to make this world a better place than before.

MG-Foundation is a Non Governmental Organization based,Helps needy and poor people, Spreads Humanity messsage

Charity without expectations. It stands for expressing unconditional love, or love without expectations.spreding positivity amongs the peoples about the helpless poples.

Our mission is to give the best of our towars the pople who are helpless,and poor. Our prime mission is give back support to "INDIA". We work towards a developed, healthy, flourishing, clean and prosperous India.
